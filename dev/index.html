Welcome to the Ying Lab

At the Ying Lab, we design next-generation soft medical devices that seamlessly interface with biological tissues for diagnostics and therapeutics. Our interdisciplinary team combines mechanical engineering, bioelectronics, and translational medicine to tackle real-world clinical challenges.

Our current projects explore novel electroadhesive hydrogels for long-term mucosal sensing, ingestible bioelectronics for gastrointestinal health, and soft robotic systems for minimally invasive interventions.
